# Maintenance Plan

## Strategy
Incremental updates with thorough testing.

## Update Frequency
Monthly, or as significant updates are available.

## Rules
- All changes must be reviewed by at least one other contributor.
- Backward compatibility must be maintained.
- Performance and readability improvements are prioritized.

## Plan
- Short-term: Integrate additional transformer models for comparison.
- Mid-term: Develop a web interface for easier interaction with the model.
- Long-term: Investigate the application of in-context learning to other areas of AI, such as computer vision.
